Integrity in Tourism

AvantGarde Vacations is an active member of the United States Tour Operators Association (USTOA). The USTOA $1 Million Travelers Assistance Program protects consumers for up to $1,000,000 in the aggregate from loss of deposits and payments for tours or vacation packages in the event of a USTOA Active Member bankruptcy, insolvency or cessation of business or the material failure to complete performance of tour(s) or vacation package(s).
