New Branding Reflects Commitment to Elevated, Private-Guided Travel Experiences New York, NY — 11/12/24 — AVANTGARDE VACATIONS, formerly known as Holidays with Downtown, announces a major rebranding as it embarks on a new chapter in delivering premium, personalized travel experiences. With a renewed focus on private-guided travel, the company has unveiled a bold new brand identity, which includes a fresh logo, redesigned website, and an enhanced social media presence. "We are incredibly excited to introduce the new brand identity," said Steven Borukhin, CDO of AVANTGARDE VACATIONS. "This transformation reflects a deep commitment to both our customers and our team. As we continue to build on our legacy of service excellence, this rebrand positions us to offer more tailored experiences, expand into emerging destinations, and connect with new segments of travelers seeking authenticity and exclusivity. It's an evolution that allows us to better reflect who we are today and where we're headed, as we continue to innovate and grow within a competitive market." "Private-Guided Journeys that Exceed Expectations" "We’re redefining what it means to offer truly personalized travel experiences," said Kevin Hichour, General Manager of AVANTGARDE.
